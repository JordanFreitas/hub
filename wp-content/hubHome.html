Get Involved

Registered Student Organizations (RSOs)

  • Joining an RSO is easy, we suggest searching the RSO Community and contact the group admins listed.
  • With over 900 RSOs registering annually there are no shortage of opportunities.
  • Students may start a new club for any new interest, specialty, or academic programs that don’t already exist.
  • Participation in an RSO is a great way for students to find their passion, work with others, develop projects and events, and add to their skill sets
  • It is also one of the most accessible ways to take what’s learned in the classroom and apply it in a live setting.
